IMPORTANT – Notice that in the example M-3 ‘auto’ keyword is used, while in example M-4 ‘auto&’ keyword is used. The difference between both keywords are –

  • auto keyword is used when you want to work with copies.
  • auto& keyword is used when you want to work with original items and may modify them.
  • Choose auto const &x when you want to work with original items and will not modify them.
